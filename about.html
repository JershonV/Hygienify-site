Our Mission.

Building a healthier St. Louis through character-led public health education and essential resource distribution.

Leadership

Jershon Vinodh

Founder and Executive Director. A high school student and community leader dedicated to public health equity in St. Louis. Jershon launched Hygienify to bridge the gap between character education and physical well-being.

Through partnerships with other non-profits, he works to ensure that the foundation for a better future is built on both strong values and good health.

He enjoys playing tennis and chess, while also hosting his own motivational seminars. Participating in LEAD Parkway & HOSA & Debate, he has strong communication and leadership skills.

"It's easy to raise an A+ student, but hard to raise a student of character."

Jershon's Guiding Principle
