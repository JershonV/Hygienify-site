Regulatory Policy Notice

501(c)(3) Tax Status Status

Hygienify is a registered non-profit corporate entity in the State of Missouri and is currently preparing and submitting its formal IRS Form 1023 Application for recognition of federal tax exemption under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Upon federal programmatic approval, tax-exempt designation is traditionally applied retroactively by the IRS back to the corporate inception date.

Donor Substantiation Rule

No Premium Exchanges

In strict accordance with IRS disclosure mandates, all capital allocations given through this portal constitute direct philanthropic public support. No commercial products, physical goods, services, admissions, or other intangible private benefits are rendered, bartered, or distributed in exchange for financial contributions made to Hygienify.
