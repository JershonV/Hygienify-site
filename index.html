The Mission

Jershon Vinodh, a Parkway West High student from Manchester, MO, launched Hygienify to combine his love for outdoors and mentorship. A tennis and chess enthusiast, Jershon focuses on building character alongside health, ensuring younger students have the tools to succeed and the hygiene to stay safe.
